About the system

Attendance monitoring, built by the section it serves

BSIT - 1C is a first-year section within the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program. To keep attendance accurate and easy to manage, the class runs on its own online attendance monitoring system.

Instead of paper logs, attendance is recorded and tracked digitally in a shared dashboard — giving the whole class a clear, always up-to-date record of who's present, every day.
